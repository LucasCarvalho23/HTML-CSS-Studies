Storm Is One of the Strongest Ever to Hit Florida

Emergency services were hampered as the storm tore across the state, bringing severe winds, storm surge and dumping as much as a foot of rain on cities. More than two million customers were without power as the storm made its way toward the Atlantic Ocean, threatening Georgia and possibly the Carolinas.
